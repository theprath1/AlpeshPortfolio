Platform teams do not need maximum flexibility everywhere. They need a path that lets them deploy, monitor, and recover systems without constantly negotiating around hidden complexity.

The most effective cloud decisions are the ones that reduce operational burden rather than shift it around. That means being deliberate about which patterns to scale and which to avoid.

## Key takeaways

- Prefer a smaller number of operationally clear patterns over broad infrastructure sprawl.
- Make tradeoffs explicit around cost, reliability, and maintenance burden before scaling a pattern.
- Choose services and abstractions that your team can support confidently during incidents.
